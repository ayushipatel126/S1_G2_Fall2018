About us

With over 50 years of combined experience, the Partners and Principals of Conrad LLP bring a tradition of excellence and dedication to a wide variety of services and client types. Our approach is to guide you through the process, not shelter you from it. We’re there for you year-round, and we’re proactive by making recommendations, watching deadlines, and completing projects long before they are due. We rely on our talented people and a heavy investment in tools and training to improve the quality of your financial reporting. We leverage technology to provide services on a real-time basis, operating in a paperless/wireless environment so that we can work as efficiently in your offices as we do in our own. Our team includes seasoned professionals that bring experience from both the private sector and global accounting firms.
